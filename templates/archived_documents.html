About Archiving
What is archiving?

Archiving allows you to keep documents that are no longer actively needed while keeping your main document list clean and organized. Archived documents are still searchable and accessible when needed.

Archiving tips
  • Archive completed projects or outdated documents
  • Archive documents that have been superseded by newer versions
  • You can unarchive documents at any time if they become relevant again
